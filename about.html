Our Journey

1990 - Foundation

Established with 200 students and 3 departments in downtown campus

  • First classes in Business, Engineering, and Liberal Arts
  • Initial faculty of 15 professors

2000 - Expansion

Expanded to 10 departments and constructed new science labs

  • Introduced first postgraduate programs
  • Established international student exchange program

2010 - Global Reach

Opened School of Business and secured global academic partnerships

  • Established 25+ international collaborations
  • Launched online learning platform

2020 - Excellence

Achieved Tier-1 national university ranking with over 10,000 students enrolled

  • Ranked #1 in research output
  • Opened new sustainable campus
